ISKOpe

About Us

Welcome to ISKOpe, a lost and found platform designed exclusively for the students of Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Manila. Inspired by the Filipino term “iskolar,” representing PUP scholars, and “scope,” meaning to search or look, ISKOpe reflects its mission to assist students in locating and reclaiming their valuables.

At ISKOpe, we understand how common it is to misplace personal belongings on a bustling university campus. With that, ISKOpe is created to be an accessible and user-friendly system to help PUPians report, search, and recover lost items with ease. Beyond its practical purpose, ISKOpe promotes a sense of trust and responsibility within the university community, making it easier to reunite students with their belongings efficiently and responsibly.
