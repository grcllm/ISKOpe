About Us

Welcome to ISKOpe, a dedicated lost-and-found system tailored for the Polytechnic University of the Philippines community.

Our goal is to connect owners with their lost items seamlessly and effectively, fostering trust and reliability within our network.

At ISKOpe, we believe in the power of community and innovation to make everyday challenges manageable. Whether you're looking to find or report a lost item, we've got you covered.
